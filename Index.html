About AI & Data Science

Artificial Intelligence and Data science together form the backbone of modern technology. Data Science focuses on collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data, while AI uses that data to learn , make predictions, and automate decision. Together, they drive innovations like personalized recommendations, intelligent chatbots, predictive healthcare, and self-driving cars.

  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning foundations
  • Data engineering, visualization, and analytics
  • Ethics, fairness, and responsible AI practices
  • Cloud-native MLOps and model deployment
